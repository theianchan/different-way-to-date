What's so different about it?

Modern romance can be a lot of work.

And that work can be fun, sometimes, and you're actually pretty good at it, if you do say so yourself.

But sending pizza heart GIFs, making dinner reservations (in this city!), keeping a running summary of trending topics and taboos, only to have to fake a work emergency in the middle of El Techo, again, because your date is getting an unsettingly intense gleam in their eye as they discuss the idea of Californian secession, again.

Sometimes, you want someone else to just take care of it

You're in luck.

We screen the dates, pick the venue, and plan the night.

No flakes, no creeps, no catfish. Our seasoned social media stalkers comb Instagram, Medium, Gawker, Venmo, real life for the most interesting and attractive, single people in the city.

All you have to do... is show up.
